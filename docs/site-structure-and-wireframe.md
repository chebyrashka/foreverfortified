## Homepage Wireframe

### 1. Hero

Purpose: Establish the brand promise immediately and drive quote requests.

Content:
- Large full-bleed home exterior or high-end remodel photo
- Headline: "Exterior and remodeling work built to hold up."
- Supporting copy: "Forever Fortified helps homeowners improve, protect, and refresh their homes with roofing, siding, and bathroom remodel services."
- Primary CTA: "Request a Free Estimate"
- Secondary CTA: "Explore Services"

Notes:
- Avoid a generic construction stock image.
- The image should show a real home or finished detail with enough clarity to inspect the quality.
- Keep the copy direct and confident.

### 2. Trust Strip

Purpose: Answer the first trust questions before the user scrolls too far.

Content ideas:
- Locally owned
- Free estimates
- Roofing, siding, and bathroom remodels
- Licensed and insured if true
- Serving Minnesota homeowners if accurate

### 3. Core Services

Purpose: Send visitors to the right conversion path.

Cards or full-width service panels:
- Roofing: inspections, repairs, replacements, weather protection
- Siding: exterior refresh, durability, curb appeal, lower maintenance
- Bathroom Remodel: comfort, function, finishes, storage, accessibility

Each service should have a short benefit-led description and a CTA to its dedicated page.

### 4. Why Forever Fortified

Purpose: Explain what makes the company credible beyond the service list.

Content themes:
- Built for Minnesota weather
- Clear communication
- Respect for the home
- Practical recommendations
- Clean craftsmanship
- Materials chosen for long-term value

### 5. Featured Project or Before/After

Purpose: Make the work tangible.

Content:
- One strong project story or before/after comparison
- Project type
- City/location if available
- Short summary of the problem and outcome
- CTA: "View Recent Work"

### 6. Testimonials

Purpose: Social proof near the middle of the decision journey.

Content:
- 3 featured testimonials
- Customer first name or initials
- City if available
- Service type
- Optional star rating

Keep this section visually substantial. Testimonials should feel like proof, not filler.

### 7. Process

Purpose: Reduce uncertainty and make contacting the company feel easy.

Steps:
- Request an estimate
- Home visit or consultation
- Clear proposal
- Build or remodel
- Final walkthrough

### 8. Articles / Homeowner Guides

Purpose: Add depth, SEO value, and helpful authority.

Example article topics:
- "How to know when your roof needs more than a repair"
- "What to consider before replacing siding"
- "Bathroom remodel choices that improve daily comfort"

### 9. Final CTA

Purpose: Close the page with a confident quote request.

Content:
- Headline: "Ready to fortify your home?"
- Supporting copy: "Tell us what you are planning and we will help you take the next clear step."
- Short estimate form or CTA to contact page

## Storyblok Content Model Implications

Build the homepage from reusable page sections:

- `hero_section`
- `trust_strip`
- `service_cards`
- `feature_section`
- `project_spotlight`
- `testimonial_grid`
- `process_steps`
- `article_teasers`
- `cta_band`

This keeps the design flexible while preventing editors from breaking layout quality.
